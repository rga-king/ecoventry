Elizabeth Coventry is an award-winning artist who specialises in botanical art.

Taking inspiration from the mountains of Andulucia in southern Spain, and her own garden in Suffolk, she produces artwork with a dedicated attention to detail.

More recently, Elizabeth has launched a range of products based on her designs.
